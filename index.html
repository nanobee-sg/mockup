Tell us your business needs

Once we know what you need, we'll match the correct nanobee influencers and manage your campaign. To start, specify the following

  • A brief backstory about your company
  • What is the product you're promoting?
  • How much total engagement do you want?

Once you've done that, our AI instantly generates an estimated campaign cost, duration and other information. We will match influencers to your campaign and notify you when the campaign posts are ready within 1 week. Our officers will vet the campaign posts for you, but you may also personally vet them.

If you feel that the posts do not align with what you have specified to us, contact us within 24 hours of the posting for us to make changes.
